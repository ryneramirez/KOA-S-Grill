KOA's Grill: A Teen Entrepreneur's Pandemic Burger Innovation


In the midst of a global pandemic that drastically altered lives and livelihoods, one teenager in the Philippines embarked on a culinary journey that would lead to the creation of Koa's Grill, a burger joint with a twist. As COVID-19 confined people to their homes and online food orders soared, our founder, a young rider looking to supplement their income, saw an opportunity to innovate. This is the story of how Koa's Grill came to be, offering burgers grilled to perfection and crafted with a commitment to quality that sets them apart in the industry.

The pandemic brought unprecedented challenges to people's lives, and Filipinos were no exception. As they adapted to lockdowns and restrictions, many turned to online food delivery services for their meals. It was during this time that our company's founder, who was already working as a rider, recognized the potential to offer something unique.

Burgers, a beloved comfort food, were the foundation of Koa's Grill. However, our founder wanted to take this classic dish and give it a twist. Instead of the conventional pan-frying method, they opted to grill the patties, infusing them with that unmistakable smoky flavor that sets them apart.

One of the key differentiators of Koa's Grill is their commitment to crafting handmade recipes. Rather than relying on mass-produced patties and generic sauces from distributors, our founder took matters into their own hands. They prepared the patties themselves and developed a unique sauce that adds an extra layer of flavor to every burger.

Burgers have earned their place as one of the world's most popular processed beef products. They are versatile, suitable for all ages, and can be enjoyed as a snack or a dinner option. Traditionally served on a bun with a medley of vegetables, cheese, and dressing, the burger's appeal is timeless.

At Koa's Grill, the emphasis is on quality. Their Grilled Burger recipe is a testament to this dedication. Using only the finest ingredients, each burger is carefully grilled to perfection, ensuring that customers are treated to a juicy and flavorful meal with every bite.
